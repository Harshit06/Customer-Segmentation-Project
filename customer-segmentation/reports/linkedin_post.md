🛍️ **From raw data to a business-ready customer strategy — my latest project!**

I built an end-to-end **Customer Segmentation** system that turns a real e-commerce customer
dataset into four clear, actionable marketing personas — and I'm excited to share it as part of
my internship portfolio.

**What I did:**
🔹 Sourced real customer data (Kaggle) and engineered it into a richer, RFM-ready dataset
🔹 Ran full EDA — distributions, outliers, correlation analysis
🔹 Built RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) scoring from scratch
🔹 Compared 3 clustering algorithms — K-Means, Hierarchical, and DBSCAN — and evaluated them with
Silhouette Score + Davies-Bouldin Index (not just picking the first one that worked!)
🔹 Used PCA to visualize customer segments in 2D/3D
🔹 Named and profiled 4 real, distinct personas: **Premium Spenders**, **Steady Mature
Regulars**, **New Bargain Hunters**, and **At-Risk Customers**
🔹 Built an interactive **Streamlit dashboard** with live filters and KPIs
🔹 Wrote a client-style executive summary translating the analysis into marketing actions

**The most interesting finding?** My "At-Risk" segment has relatively *high* income but the
lowest engagement — meaning affordability isn't the problem, disengagement is. That single
insight completely changes what a win-back campaign should look like.

This project pushed me to think beyond "just run K-Means" — comparing algorithms, checking
cluster stability, and translating clusters into business decisions was the real learning.
